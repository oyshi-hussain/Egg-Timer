Fried Egg Instructions

  1. Heat a non-stick pan over medium-low heat and melt a small amount of butter or oil.
  2. Crack the egg into the pan, keeping the yolk intact. 🥚
  3. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the white is set.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then serve.
  5. Total time to cook the egg: About 4 minutes
